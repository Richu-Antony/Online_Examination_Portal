ABOUT US

This is a free to use online examination portal help us to coduct online examination and test to try out your skills.

Online Examination portal is a technology-driven way to simplify examination activities like defining exam patterns with question banks, defining exam timer, objective/subjective question sections, conducting exams using the computer or mobile devices in a paperless manner. It is a cost-effective, scalable way to convert traditional pen and paper-based exams to online and paperless mode.

Candidates can appear for the exam using any desktop, laptop, or mobile device with a browser. Exam results can be generated instantly for the objective type of questions.
